ISLAND ROMANCE REIMAGINED

An exclusive waterfront setting for luxurious weddings and receptions. Inspired architecture, lush surrounds and the natural exclusivity provided by both ocean and lagoon frontage sets the scene for unparalleled sophistication. Say your vows on a stretch of dramatic beach, in lush gardens, or in a formal ballroom overlooking both ocean and lagoon. Enjoy honeymoons of pool villa luxury at the best of wedding hotels in Sri Lanka.

Ballroom

With high ceilings, a neutral palette and views of both ocean and lagoon, the ballroom provides an inspiring backdrop for traditional ceremonies, formal banquets or cocktail receptions. Ideal for up to 350 guests seated or 400 for a cocktail reception.

Beach

Exchange vows with a sunset backdrop of Indian Ocean, honey-hued sand and swaying coconut trees. The secluded beach cove can be decorated as you like, with friends and family seated facing the ocean. Ideal for up to 350 guests.

Garden

Step into tropical landscaped gardens edged by the Kalu Ganga for natural exclusivity. Choose a Western-style ceremony or a traditional Sri Lankan procession with Kandyan dancers and drummers. Ideal for up to 350 guests.
